Explore Georgia’s Cultural Heritage in Augmented Reality

From ancient wine cellars to majestic fortresses, Vinorgi AR lets you experience the rich heritage of Georgia from anywhere in the world.

🏺 Step into history – View 3D models of ancient sites and artifacts.

🍷 Taste the culture – Learn about Georgia’s world-famous Saperavi wine while seeing the vineyards where it’s made.

📱 Easy access – Scan a QR code, place models in your space, and explore with your friends.

🌍 Preserve heritage – Share your experiences to support cultural preservation digitally.
